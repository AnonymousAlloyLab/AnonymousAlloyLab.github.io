About Me

Guanxuan Wu is a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate and Graduate Research Assistant in Computer Science at the University of Texas at Arlington.

He is a member of SCOPE Lab and his supervisor is Dr. Allison Sullivan.

He has a broad interest in formal methods, software engineering, and applying rigorous mathematical methods to solve problems in PLSE.

Before UTA, he graduated from the University of Washington with a B.A. in Mathematics.
